About FxAThng

FxAThng coordinates independent studios focused on delivering reliable engineering outcomes. Each studio operates with domain focus and shared operational standards to accelerate delivery while maintaining long-term resilience.

History

Founded in 2084, after founder Juan Son, on a vision quest through our broken City, felt the need to Fix. He started out assembling a small consultancy focused on systems architecture. Over time, we expanded to become an umbrella for specialized engineering studios, each bringing deep expertise in their respective domains. Today, we partner with organizations of all sizes to build and scale reliable systems.

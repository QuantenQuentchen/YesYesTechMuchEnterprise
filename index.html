About FxAThng

FxAThng is an umbrella organization that brings together specialized engineering studios. Each studio focuses on a specific domain, from systems and platform engineering to product design and operational excellence.

Active studios:

  • Systems Engineering Solutions — distributed systems, architecture, and SRE practices.
  • Platform Integration Group — APIs, middleware, and integration tooling.
  • Product Velocity Labs — product strategy, rapid prototyping, and UX engineering.
  • Observability & Operations — telemetry, incident readiness, and runbooks.
